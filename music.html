About

I trained in Hindustani classical music for four years, with a focus on voice development — the vibrato and pitch control in my singing comes directly from that training. Since then, I've written original songs, performed live, and recorded covers across piano, guitar, and voice. This page is a small selection of that work, shared alongside my acoustics research because the two have always informed each other: an ear trained on pitch and timbre is not so different from an ear trained on spectral analysis.
